Chinese Mythological Characters

  • Chang'e - Moon goddess who drank immortality elixir.
  • Hou Yi - Archer who shot down nine suns.
  • Jingwei - Bird vowing to fill the sea.
  • Kuafu - Giant chasing the sun to its setting.
  • Mazu - Sea Goddess safeguarding seafarers.
  • Nezha - Youthful deity battling dragons.
  • Nüwa - Creator goddess who molded humans.
  • Pangu - Cosmic giant birthing the world.
